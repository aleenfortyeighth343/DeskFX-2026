## 📉 Advanced audio configuration

For better sound staging, use the environment settings. These presets emulate different acoustic spaces. Choosing a room preset adjusts the timing of audio reflections to mimic a specific location. Use these for gaming or watching movies to create a sense of scale.

The gain control manages the overall volume level. Keep this level in the safe zone to prevent clipping. Clipping occurs when the digital signal reaches its limit and causes crackling or popping noises in your speakers. If you hear distortion, lower the gain settings immediately.

Ensure your Windows sound settings are set to high quality as well. Right-click the speaker icon in your taskbar, select Sound settings, and ensure your output device is set to at least 24-bit, 48000 Hz. DeskFX functions best when the source material is clean and high-fidelity.
